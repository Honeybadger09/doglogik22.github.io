We teach humans new tricks

Doglogik uses reward-based dog training techniques and gentle correction to enforce good behaviour and promote responsible dog ownership.

Run by professional dog trainer Jan Giebelmann, we teach you how to train your dog using force-free methods that reward appropriate behaviours instead of punishing negative ones.

They bring the tricks. You bring the treats.

Consistent, positive interactions between you and your dog build trust and enhance their overall mental, physical, and social wellbeing.

That’s why we use common sense concepts and classical conditioning methods that make dog training easy and effective.

RugnTug dog toys

Play forms an integral part of my dog training approach.

Puppies learn when they’re having fun, while games and activities keep your adult dog’s mind active and engaged.

And there’s nothing like a game of tug or fetch.

That’s why I created RugnTug Toys, a range of hand-made, high-quality rope dog toys, including tugs and balls, as well as custom-made slip-leads.

They’re great tools for positive reinforcement training, and give you and your pooch a fun, free workout as a bonus.
